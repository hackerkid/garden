how to read books

tags: #books #reading #note-taking #public

  • First go through the index of the book in the back
    • Note down the topics that are interesting to you.
  • Get a sense of what the major topics are and what the author is trying to convey
  • Try to formulate an on idea on what the book is about
  • Go to the table of contents
    • See how close you were on guessing what the book is about
  • Start reading the topics you are interested in
  • Take notes using the highlighter
  • Export the notes using anki, readwise etc
  • If you dont find the book compelling enough, keep back in the shelf and read it again when you find the need.
