Products that are viral by nature

tags: #startup #sideproject #ideas #viral #public

  • buffer - Each and every tweet posted through buffer introduces a lot of people to buffer
  • threader -Each and every time a user mentions threader it introduces a lot of people to threader
  • zoom - Each and every call a user make with someone introduces one or more person to Zoom.
  • calendy - Each and every meeting a user schedules introduces someone to calendy.
  • mailchimp - Each and every newsletter a user sends introduces a lot of users to mailchimp
  • google form - Each and every form introduces a lot of new users to Google form. *
